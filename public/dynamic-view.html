Threshold Help

Dynamic View

Dynamic View is where you’ll be doing the majority of your photo organization. From here you’ll be able to create rooms on your plans, organize your photos by rooms, and walk your jobsite virtually.

View Unassigned Photos

Your photos will be displayed by default in the unassigned window on the left side of Dynamic View.

Create/ Edit Rooms

Unassigned photos need to be assigned to a specific location on your plans. Threshold makes this easy by allowing you to create rooms right on your plan sheets.

Create Rooms

To create a room right click on the desired location on the plan. A drop down will appear, select "add room" from the list. You can right click anywhere on the plan to designate a room. Fill in the name of the specified room and hit "submit". Complete this action for each room on your plan.

Tip: Name the rooms the same as what is printed on the Architectural plans

Edit Rooms

To change the location of a room, right click on the room icon. In the drop down, click on "move room". The room icon will turn green with an unlocked icon, then click and drag the room icon to the desired location.

To rename a room, right click on the room icon. In the drop down, click on "rename room". Enter the new name in the window and select submit.

To delete a room, right click on the room icon. In the drop down, click on "delete room".

Tip: All photos that are associated with the deleted room will be returned to the Unassigned photo bank. The photos themselves will NOT be deleted.

Assign Photos

To add photos to your plans they MUST be dropped onto a room. To learn how to create rooms view the previous section.

Click on the "Photos" button to show and hide the unassigned photos toolbar.

Drag and Drop

The unassigned photo bank on the left side of the page displays all photos that have not been assigned to any specific location in the project. You can place a photo into a room in two ways, either individually or by selecting multiple photos at once.

To place a single photo into a room, simply drag the photo from the photo bank and release it over the desired room icon.

To place multiple photos into a room, click on the check mark on the bottom right of the photos. Once you have selected the photos, drag the photos from the photo bank and release them over the desired room icon.

The room icon will change to show you that you’re dropping the photos in the room.

Introduction to Tagging

When you place a photo in a room, it's being tagged with the plan type you're currently viewing. These plan types were specified in Plan Management. A photo placed by dragging and dropping on a plan sheet will only be visible on that specific plan sheet unless you’ve added multiple tags to the photo.

You can learn more about this in the Tagging and Photo Viewer help sections.

Set and Plan Type Drop Down

Use the set and plan type drop downs to switch between sets and plan types that your photos are associated with.

View Photos by Room

Once your photos have been added to a room, click on the room icons to view them. You can tell which rooms have photos by the blue color of the icon. The number on the icon indicates the number of photos in that room.

Tip: As a reminder, the number shown on the room icon represents only photos tagged with a specific trade. To view all photos in a room, use the Gallery View.
