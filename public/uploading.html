Uploading

In this section you'll learn how to upload photos and plans. We will not be covering the mobile app in this section.

Upload Plans

Note: You can only upload .pdf plans and they MUST be single-page .pdfs.

Uploading Plans

You can upload your plans from your project dashboard or plan management.

Select Plans

Plans must be in single-page .pdf format. If you have a set of plans that are in one file break the plans into individual pages. If you need assistance with this, please visit this link or contact us at support@383studio.com for additional help.

Uploading

If youâ€™re using Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Apple Safari you can drag files from a folder directly into the box with a dashed line around it or anywhere else on the page. If you're using Internet Explorer you'll need to hit the orange "upload plans" button to browse files on your computer.

You can upload multiple plans at once. To do so, hold the Shift key and select multiple. You can also select individual files by holding the Ctrl key (command on a Mac).

View your Plans

Your plans will be displayed on the right side of the upload plan page They will appear after the file has finished uploading and processing. You can go through the thumbnails and remove any files mistakenly uploaded.

Note: Uploading plans is an intensive process and can take a while. The site is taking your .pdf file and converting it into a file format that Threshold can use. This can take up to a few minutes per plan but generally much less.

Next Step

After your plans have been uploaded you can use the navigation buttons on the bottom left of the plan upload page to go to plan management or project dashboard. To continue setting up your project go to plan management.

Upload Photos

You can upload photos from either the project dashboard or inside dynamic view.

Uploading

If you're using Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Apple Safari you can drag files from a folder directly into the box with a dashed line around it or anywhere else on the page.

If you're using Internet Explorer you'll need to hit the orange "upload plans" button to browse files on your computer. You can upload multiple plans at one time. To do so hold the Shift key and select all the plans you want to upload. You can also select individual files by holding the Ctrl key (command on a Mac).

View your Photos

Your photos will be displayed on the right side of the upload photo page. They will appear after the file has finished uploading and processing. You can go through the thumbnails and remove any files mistakenly uploaded.

After your photos have been uploaded you can use the navigation buttons on the bottom left of the upload page to go dynamic view, gallery, or dashboard.
